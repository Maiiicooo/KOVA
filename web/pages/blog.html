March 19, 2026 — Full UI rebrand

KOVA just went through its biggest visual update so far. The old look is gone, with a full rebrand across the platform, new logos built from scratch, and a completely redesigned map interface.

The new X logo is a direct nod to “X marks the spot” — a simple symbol for discovery, hidden places, and spots worth finding.

🚀 New features
  • Introduced a full new visual identity across the platform.
  • Replaced the previous logo system with brand new logos made from scratch.
  • Redesigned the map UI from the ground up for a cleaner and more immersive feel.
  • Updated overall layout styling to better match the new KOVA direction.

This update is less about adding one feature and more about redefining how KOVA feels as a whole.
