About KOVA

KOVA is a digital outdoor utility built for people who like being outside without overplanning it. It’s about finding quiet places, overlooked corners, and hidden gems. Mapped by real people, not algorithms.

The idea started from a simple frustration: good places are hard to find unless someone tells you. KOVA exists to collect those places and put them on a map, without turning them into tourist traps.

KOVA is intentionally minimal. No feeds screaming for attention. No endless notifications. Just a map, some context, and a growing community that knows when to keep things low-key.

The project is currently in alpha. Features are evolving, the map is growing, and some things may break — that’s part of the process.

Built and maintained by @maicomargiela.
