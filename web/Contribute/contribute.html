Contribute to Kova

Kova is still in its early alpha stage — a tiny, independent side-project built to make discovering great spots simple, real, and community-driven. Right now it’s just me designing everything and writing every line of code.

The idea is growing faster than expected, and I genuinely need help from people who care about good spots, clean design, and honest tools.

How you can help
