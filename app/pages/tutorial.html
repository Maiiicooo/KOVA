Quick guide for the radar view and the map view.

Radar view

The radar layer shows precipitation intensity. Stronger color = heavier rain.

Blue
Light rain
Green
Moderate rain
Yellow
Heavy showers
Orange
Storm
Radar is an estimate (reflection-based). Wind and timing can shift rain. Use it as awareness, not as a guarantee.

Map view

The map lets you explore spots and interact with any point on the map.

  • Click a spot → opens the spot popup with photo and info
  • Click anywhere on the map → loads spots in a 10 km radius around that point
  • Use an empty point → also opens the add spot popup at that exact location
Tip: every point on the map can act as a search center. Empty points can also be used to start adding a new spot.
