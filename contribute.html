Contribute to Kova

Kova is a small, independent project built to make discovering great spots easier and more honest. Right now it’s just me building everything — design, code, and the entire map.

The community is growing fast, and I could use your help.

What helps most:

Adding new spots

Share locations that others should know about and help the map grow.

Design input

Small UI touches, icons, visuals, or creative ideas that make Kova feel better.

General feedback

Thoughts on features, flow, or what Kova should become next.

If you want to contribute, reach out at hello@kova.spot or submit a spot directly through the app.

Kova gets better because people help build it.
